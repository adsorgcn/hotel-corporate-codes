## 💡 Pro Tips

**Average Room Rate Theory** — The bigger the gap between your corporate rate and the hotel's ADR, the more likely they verify ID. Tier-1 cities = more checks.

**Always Compare** — Corporate rates aren't always cheapest. Weekend promotions and flash sales can beat them. Always compare before booking.

**Full Loyalty Points** — Corporate rate bookings earn full loyalty points and count toward elite status at all major chains. This is confirmed policy, not a loophole.

**If Asked for ID** — Worst case: you pay the regular rate. No penalty, no blacklist.
